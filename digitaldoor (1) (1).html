FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your project.

How long does it take to build my website?

Most projects go live within 7 days — from our first discovery call to launch and WhatsApp setup. Simpler sites can be ready even faster.

Do I need to pay everything upfront?

We typically take 50% to start and 50% on delivery. No hidden fees — the price on our pricing page is what you pay.

Can I update my products and prices myself?

Yes. We include a full handover training session so you can manage your catalogue, images, and content without depending on us.

Will my site work on mobile phones?

Every site we build is mobile-first. Most of your customers in Karachi browse and order on WhatsApp from their phones — we design for that.

Do you help with WhatsApp ordering?

Absolutely. WhatsApp integration is a core part of what we do — order buttons, pre-filled messages, and flows that turn visitors into customers instantly.
